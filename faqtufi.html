Tamil Nadu Urban Finance and Infrastructure Development Corporation LTD (TUFIDCO).

• Additional Chief Secretary / Principal Secretary / Secretary, Municipal Administration and Water Supply Department - Chairperson / Chairman.
• Additional Chief Secretary, Finance Department or his representative - Member.
• Commissioner, Greater Chennai Corporation - Member.
• Managing Director, Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Board - Member.
• Chairman and Managing Director / Managing Director, Tamil Nadu Urban Finance and Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited – Convener.

• Approval of the Singara Chennai 2.0 Plan.
• Prioritization of projects to be posed for funding.
• Prescribing appraisal criteria.
• Approval of the projects and according Administrative Sanction for the projects.
• Approving the cost of the technical assistance.
• Deciding the means of financing for the projects viz. loan/grant component or proportion of viability gap funding etc.,
• Deciding on the fund release pattern to the implementing agencies.
• Reviewing and monitoring the progress of the projects duringimplementation and providing critical decisions, wherever required. The Committee will meet at a periodicity to be decided by it.

The Commissioner, Greater Chennai Corporation and the Managing Director, Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Board ,with the other technical export will monitor the implementation of project.
